About Us

HEBEI HAOQI DRILLING EQUIPMENT provide premium rock drill bits. Since 2004, we’ve provided the highest quality tools at the best prices, allowing our clients to stay ahead of schedule and on budget. We supply the industry with quality tools such as Tricone bit, PDC bit, Rock reamer, PDC hole opener,HDD Drilling Tools, We ship to 82 different countries, serving industries such as construction, oil and gas, mining, horizontal directional drilling (HDD), hydraulic, geothermal and environmental. Our experienced staff is here to help you with everything from selecting the right drill bit to shipping worldwide.
