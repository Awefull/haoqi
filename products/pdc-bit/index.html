The Advantage of PDC Core Bit

The original purpose of polycrystalline diamond composite (PDC) core bits was for oil drilling. High-quality synthetic diamond particles are fused together by high pressure and temperature to form the surface of the cylindrical PDC cutters. The PDC core bits are used extensively in the oil, gas, mining, and engineering drilling areas and have high tool hardness, wear resistance, and toughness.

PDC core bits offer significantly higher drilling life and penetration than surface fixed bits, making them ideal for use with high-power drilling rigs moving at relatively slow speeds.
